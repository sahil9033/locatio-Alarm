At Location Alarm, your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

🔐 What We Collect
We may collect the following data to provide core functionality:
- Location Data: Used to trigger alarms based on your selected destinations. This data is processed locally and never stored or shared.
- Device Information: Basic device data (e.g., OS version) may be used to optimize performance.
- Usage Analytics (Optional): Anonymous data may be collected to improve app features. You can opt out anytime.

🚫 What We Don’t Do
- We do not track your location continuously.
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
- We do not access your contacts, media, or other personal files.

🧠 How Your Data Is Used
- Location triggers: Your location is used only when the app is active and alarms are set.
- Performance optimization: Device info helps us ensure smooth operation across Android versions.
- User feedback: Optional analytics help us refine the experience.

🛡️ Data Security
We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. All location processing happens locally on your device. No cloud storage or external servers are involved unless explicitly stated.

📢 Ads & Monetization
If ads are displayed, they are served via trusted platforms (e.g., AdSense) and may use anonymized data for relevance. You can learn more or adjust preferences via the app settings.

👤 Your Control
- You can disable location access anytime via your device settings.
- You can opt out of analytics and personalized ads.
- You can uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.
